Happy Earth Day? More Like Happy Earth Yay!

April 22nd is International Earth Day!

The earth is equally your home as it is everyone else's, love it, repect it and treat it right :)

Some great things you can do to celebrate are...

  • Use less electricity for one hour of the day, for ecample try turning off some lights and unplugging some unused chargers! You can even go full sicko mode and go full blackout mode !
  • Don't get takeout with single-use containers and cutlery, instead, bring you own containers, forks and spoons.
  • Use a metal straw with that starnucks drink instead of a plastic one!
  • Do some gardening! Snatch that carbon dioxide out of the atmosphere!
